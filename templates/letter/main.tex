	\medskip

	I am writing to recommend Benjamín Vera for the PhD fellowship position at INRIA INOCS, related to Game Theory for Energy System Decarbonization. I had the opportunity to supervise Benjamín during his master’s studies, and I also worked with him in my role as a lecturer, where he served as a teaching assistant in two courses—one focused on the theory of Convex Analysis, and the other on Nonlinear Optimization with a more practical orientation.

	During his master's thesis on the existence and computation of Nash equilibria in electrical markets, Benjamín demonstrated a solid grasp of optimization theory and algorithms, as well as a strong motivation to engage with the game-theoretic aspects of the topic, which he acquired along the way. He approached his work with diligence and intellectual curiosity and has been able to carry out his thesis without significant difficulty. His background in both the theoretical and applied aspects of nonlinear optimization makes him well prepared for the demands of a doctoral program in this area. Furthermore, he has shown good capacities for computational implementations of different algorithms for computing equilibria.

	As a teaching assistant, Benjamín was reliable and clear in his explanations, helping students to navigate challenging material. His enthusiasm for real-world applications of optimization was evident throughout our work, and I believe this motivation will serve him well in a research environment focused on practical impact.

	I think he would be a capable and committed addition to your PhD position, and thus I strongly support his application without any hesitation.

	\medskip
	Sincerely, \\
